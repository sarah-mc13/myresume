SUBARU Impreza STI 22B

The 22B STI was made as part of a homologation rule for a group A rally class Subaru participating in. The rule made it so 425 22B STI's were made. It incorporated a flat 4 turbo charged engine and 4 wheel drive.

1972 (Hakosuka) Skyline GTR

The '72 Skyline GTR, also known as the Hakosuka, sported a inline 6 engine with dual overhead cams and 4 valves per cylinder which was impressive tehcnology in that era. It made a whopping 160HP and 113 lb-ft torque.

1997 Toyota A90 Supra

THe Supra came with a legendary inline six cylinder engine known as the 2JZ, a 2.2 that had an optional twin turbo version making a "276HP". actuall numbers are closer to 300HP but were said to be lower due to a gentlemans agrement made by japanese automakers. It is highly saught after as a tuners car.

1985 Honda CRX

The CRX has an inline 4 cylinder engine making 91HP and 84 lb-ft of torque which, by itself, is unimpressive but paired with a chasis that weights under 1,900 lbs made the chasis feel "sporty" and quick. Handling is nimble.

1987 Mazda rx7 Turbo II

The turbo II came with a 2 rotor 13B engine. Turbo charged with air feeding through its iconic hoodscoop. it produces 178 brake HP and 182 lb-ft of torque. Put in a light wieght chasis making a "sporty" platform.
